Practice

Lee Architecture provides rigorous design and technical leadership for the built environment.

With over 14 years of industry experience, the practice is led by a principal with a proven track record in delivering complex, large-scale developments. Previously serving as a Senior Associate at Woods Bagot and Rothelowman, the practice brings institutional-level expertise to a more agile, client-focused model.

Our portfolio spans multi-residential, commercial, and health sectors. This expertise is bolstered by significant international experience across Asia, specifically in Japan and South Korea, where we have managed projects requiring high levels of technical precision and cultural nuance.
